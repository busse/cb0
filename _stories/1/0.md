This is the intent story (s0) for idea 1. Every idea should have an s0 story that explains:

## Why This Idea Exists

This sample idea demonstrates the taxonomy system in action. It shows how ideas, stories, and sprints work together.

## What Problem It Solves

By providing a concrete example, users can:
- See how to structure their own ideas
- Understand the relationship between ideas and stories
- Learn the notation system through example

## Expected Outcomes

Users who study this example will be able to:
1. Create their own ideas with proper front matter
2. Add stories to their ideas
3. Use the notation system correctly
